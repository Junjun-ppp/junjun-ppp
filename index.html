Interests

Control theory:

Basically, I am not interested in the control object, but in the theory. I am interested in both classical and quantum systems, but in the case of classical systems, I would like to deal with relatively large systems such as power grids and markets, rather than robots and the like.

Dynamical systems theory, nonlinear dynamics:

I am also interested in dynamical systems theory, nonlinear dynamics, and control theory. I am particularly interested in synchronous phenomena, chaos in neural networks, quantum theory and dynamical systems, and the connection of chaos.

Quantum information:

I would like to keep up to date with the latest trends. I am interested in error correction in quantum systems.
